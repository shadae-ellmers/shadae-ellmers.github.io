Emotional Intelligence

What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand and manage your own emotions for positive reactions, as well as understanding the emotions of others around you.

How is EQ different from IQ?

IQ refers to cognitive intelligence, which is based on a person’s mental ability. This can involve visual processing, working memory, or quantitative reasoning. EQ refers to emotional intelligence, and as stated above refers to a person’s ability to acknowledge, process, or control emotions. This can involve identifying emotions, relating to others, and social communication.

Why is EQ important?

Emotional intelligence is important as it allows you to build relationships with others. This is an important skill as it allows you to reduce interpersonal stress and conflict, as well as increase successful collaboration and leadership.
